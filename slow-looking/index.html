Workshop notes

Challenging the blank page

A half-day sketchbook workshop exploring mark making, visual starting points and alternative ways into drawing.

Class rhythm

10 minsWelcome. Reassure the group that the sketchbook is a place to test and collect, not to perform.
20 minsMarks without judgement: pressure, smudge, erase, repeat.
30 minsCollecting from a small detail: texture, edge, shadow, shape.
25 minsFrom detail to abstraction: enlarge one small observation across the page.
25 minsLayered response using two or three materials.
10 minsQuiet sharing: what surprised you?

Teacher notes

Keep the language permission-giving. Encourage pauses, looking again, and treating accidental marks as useful information.
“You are not trying to finish a drawing. You are collecting evidence of looking.”
“Let one accidental mark lead the next decision.”
“The sketchbook is a place to test, not to perform.”
